About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Abid Chicken Centre began with a simple mission: to serve the freshest, most delicious chicken dishes to our community. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to quality and authentic flavors.

Every day, we source the finest ingredients and prepare each dish with care and attention to detail. Our recipes have been perfected over years of dedication, combining traditional cooking methods with modern culinary techniques.
